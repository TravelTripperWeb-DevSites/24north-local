Kino’s Sandal Delivery

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock amenites_03_content, type:text %}

24 North Hotel has partnered with Kino Sandals, a world-famous sandal factory in Old Town Key West since 1966, to launch a "1-Hour Sandal Delivery" program for hotel guests.  As Jimmy Buffet once sang, it's common for vacationers to "blow out their flip flop." Now, through the front desk, hotel guests can order a pair or three of these handmade Key West souvenirs and have them delivered directly to their hotel door within one hour. Guests can also purchase the sandals pre-arrival, and have them waiting for them upon check-in.

This service is offered daily between the hours of 10 a.m. and 5:30 p.m from Monday through Saturday and 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. on Sunday. The service offers the classic thong-style sandals for children and adults for $20.43 including tax
